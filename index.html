Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about tutoring with Cloud Classroom

How do online tutoring sessions work?

We meet via Google Meet or Zoom – whichever you prefer. I'll share my screen, use a digital whiteboard, and guide you through problems step by step. All you need is a device with internet and a quiet space.

Which areas do you mostly teach?

We are available in: Athi River, Mlolongo, Kitengela, Isinya and EPZ. Any areas outside of that we can only offer Virtual lessons (*subject to negotiation).

Can we reschedule a session if something comes up?

Absolutely! Life happens. Just let us know at least 4 hours in advance and we'll find a new time that works for both of us. No penalties, no stress.

Do you offer discounts for siblings or groups?

Yes! Check out our Pricing page for the Double Plan (two students) and The Study Squad (3+ students). Group learning is effective and affordable.

What subjects and levels do you teach?

See the Subjects page for the full list.

How do I pay? Is M‑Pesa accepted?

Yes, M‑Pesa is accepted, alternately you can pay by our till number: 433250. Bank transfer is also available. We'll agree on a plan and we'll share payment details after booking.

What if I need extra help between sessions?

You can always WhatsApp us a quick question. For longer doubts, we can address them in the next session. We're here to support you, not just during scheduled hours.

Still have questions? Contact us directly – We're happy to help.
